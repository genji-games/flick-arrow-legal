# Support — Flick Arrow

Thanks for playing **Flick Arrow**. Need help, found a bug, or want to delete your leaderboard entry? Reach out below.

## How to contact us

- **Email**: [genji.games08@gmail.com](mailto:genji.games08@gmail.com)
- **Public issue tracker**: [github.com/genji-games/flick-arrow/issues](https://github.com/genji-games/flick-arrow/issues) — open an issue if you'd rather have your question seen publicly

We aim to respond within **five business days**.

## Frequently asked

### How do I edit my nickname?

Tap the "Playing as ..." chip under the Start button on the home screen, type a new name (2–16 characters), and tap **Save**. Your leaderboard row updates immediately.

### My score didn't appear on the leaderboard

Scores are only submitted to the leaderboard when you set a **new best** in that game mode. If you scored less than your previous best, the leaderboard stays at your highest. Try beating your previous best.

### How do I delete my data?

Uninstalling the app clears all locally stored data on your phone. To also remove your leaderboard row from our backend, email us with your nickname and approximate last play time. We'll remove it within 30 days, free of charge. See the [Privacy Policy](./privacy) for full details.

### What permissions does the app need?

- **iOS**: none.
- **Android**: `MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS` (auto-granted, used for sound playback).

The app does **not** access your microphone, camera, location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive data.

### Why is the timer so fast at higher scores?

By design. Past score 25, the timer per arrow drops 0.4 s every 25 points until it floors at 1.0 s after score 125. If it feels unfair, congrats — you've outgrown the warm-up.

### The app crashed / shows a "Something went wrong" screen

The screen has the error details. **Screenshot it and send it to the email above** and we'll look into it. Each report helps us fix bugs across all players.
